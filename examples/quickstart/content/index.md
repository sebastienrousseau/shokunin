## A small London coffee roastery

**Heron Coffee** is a single-origin roaster on Druid Street, Bermondsey. We roast in 12kg batches every Tuesday morning and ship within 48 hours of the roast date.

The cafe is open **Monday to Saturday, 7:30am to 4pm**. Online orders ship anywhere in the UK and Europe; allow 2-3 working days.

## This week's roasts

- **Finca La Esperanza** *(Colombia, washed)* — citrus, brown sugar, milk chocolate. Roasted 14 April.
- **Sidamo Guji** *(Ethiopia, natural)* — blueberry, jasmine, dark molasses. Roasted 14 April.
- **Cerrado** *(Brazil, pulped natural)* — hazelnut, dark cocoa, low acidity — our espresso default. Roasted 14 April.

Subscribe to a [monthly bag](/contact/) and we'll ship the same day we roast.

## Visit the cafe

**47 Druid Street, London SE1 2HQ**
*(2-minute walk from London Bridge station, behind the Maltby Street Market)*

Open Mon–Sat 7:30am–4pm. Closed Sundays.

## What we're brewing this morning

- Espresso: **Cerrado** (default) or **Finca La Esperanza** (£0.50 supplement)
- Filter: **Sidamo Guji** on V60, **Finca La Esperanza** on Chemex
- Decaf: **Mexican Chiapas** (Swiss water process), available all day
