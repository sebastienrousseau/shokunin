## Samfuran Farko

Dukkan samfuran farko, ko shafuka ne ko shafukan yanar gizo, an gina su daga farawa
ta amfani da ƙa'idodi da fasalulluka iri ɗaya. Wannan yana nufin kuna iya cakuda su
da juna cikin sauƙi don ƙirƙirar shimfidu da abun ciki na musamman.

Fara da SSG ta amfani da kowane daga cikin Samfuran Farko namu don gina
shafin yanar gizonku ko aikace-aikacen yanar gizo.

**Samfuran Farko na Kaishi** yana da fasalulluka masu zuwa:

- **Tags na Meta na Samun Dama:** An tsara waɗannan tags na meta don sanya shafin ya zama mai samun dama ga masu amfani masu nakasa. Ta hanyar saita matsayin Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA), cikakken ikon keyboard, da babu haɗarin walƙiya, kuna iya tabbatar da cewa shafinku yana samun dama ga kowa.
- **Tags na Meta na Apple:** Waɗannan tags na meta suna inganta shafuka don na'urorin Apple, kamar iPhones, iPads, da sauran na'urorin Apple. Kuna iya canza ƙarfin web app, salon status bar, take, sunan aikace-aikacen, da marubuci don inganta bayyanar na'urorin Apple.
- **Bootstrap CSS:** Bootstrap shahararren tsarin CSS ne wanda ke ba ku saitin salo da sassan da aka riga aka tsara. Ta amfani da Bootstrap, kuna iya ƙirƙirar shafin yanar gizo mai kama da ƙwararru cikin sauri ba tare da rubuta CSS daga farawa ba.
- **Bootstrap JavaScript:** Bootstrap JavaScript saitin rubutun da aka riga aka gina wanda ke ba ku jerin kewayawa masu dacewa da kuma akwatunan tattaunawa na modal.
- **Manufar Tsaron Abun Ciki:** Ana amfani da wannan tag na meta don tantance tushen abun cikin da aka ba da izini a loda shi a shafin. An tsara shi don hana hare-haren XSS da sauran rashin tsaro.
- **Tags na Meta na Microsoft:** An tsara waɗannan tags na meta don inganta shafin don na'urorin Microsoft. Kuna iya saita tabbatar da shafin, daidaitattun aikace-aikacen, launin hasken taɓawa, launin tile, da hoton tile don yin kama mai kyau a kan na'urorin Windows.
- **Tags na Meta na Open Graph/Facebook:** Waɗannan tags na meta suna ba ku damar sarrafa yadda shafinku ya bayyana lokacin da aka raba shi a Facebook da sauran dandamali na kafofin watsa labarun. Ta hanyar saita take, bayani, da hoto, kuna iya tabbatar da cewa shafinku yana duban mafi kyau lokacin da aka raba shi a kan layi.
- **Jerin Kewayawa Mai Dacewa:** Jerin kewayawa mai dacewa yana ba masu amfani haɗin kai mai sauƙi don kewaya shafin. Yana nufin daidaita girman allo, yana mai da shi samun dama ga masu amfani a kan tebur da na'urorin hannu.
- **Tags na Meta na Schema.org:** Ana amfani da waɗannan tags na meta don samar da tsarin bayanai game da abun cikin shafin. Saita suna, bayani, da hoto akan shafin yana taimaka wa injunan bincike da sauran mutane su fahimci abun cikin sosai.
- **Tags na Meta na Twitter:** An tsara waɗannan tags na meta don inganta shafin don rabawa a Twitter. Kuna iya saita nau'in kati, mahalicci, bayani, hoto, shafin, take, da URL don sanya shafinsu ya yi kama da kyau a kan Twitter.
