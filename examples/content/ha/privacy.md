Muna amfani da sabis biyu na ɓangare na uku don sa ido da bincika zirga-zirgar yanar gizo: Google Analytics da Microsoft Clarity.

Google Analytics sabis ne na nazarin yanar gizo wanda Google ke bayarwa wanda ke bin diddigi kuma ya ba da rahoton zirga-zirgar shafin yanar gizo. Don ƙarin bayani kan ayyukan sirri na Google, da fatan za a ziyarci shafin Google Privacy & Terms: [Google Privacy & Terms ⧉](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Microsoft Clarity kayan aikin bincika halayen mai amfani ne wanda ke taimaka mana fahimtar yadda masu amfani ke hulɗa da shafinmu. Bayanan da aka tattara sun haɗa da bayanai kamar motsin linzamin kwamfuta, danne, da gungurawa. Don ƙarin bayani kan ayyukan sirri na Microsoft, da fatan za a ziyarci shafin Microsoft Privacy Statement: [Microsoft Privacy Statement ⧉](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement)
